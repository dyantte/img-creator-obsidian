# /trace — Track how an idea evolved

Track how a specific idea, concept, or topic has evolved over time across the vault. Build a historical timeline of how my thinking has changed.

**Topic:** $ARGUMENTS

## Steps

1. Search the entire vault for all mentions of this topic and related terms
2. Build a vocabulary map — what related concepts appear alongside this topic?
3. Order everything chronologically
4. Follow backlinks to find indirect connections

## Output

Structure as an evolution trace:

### First Appearance
- When and where this topic first shows up in the vault

### Evolution Timeline
For each significant phase:
- **Date range**
- **Key quotes** from the vault (exact words)
- **What shifted** from the previous phase
- **What triggered the shift** (if identifiable)

### Current State
- Where my thinking stands now on this topic

### Trajectory
- Where this seems to be heading based on the trend

### Related Concepts
- What other vault topics are most connected to this one

Use my actual words from the vault whenever possible. This is about showing me my own thinking, not Claude's interpretation of it.
