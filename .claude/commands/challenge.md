# /challenge — Pressure test a belief

Pressure test my current thinking on a topic using the vault's own history. Find contradictions, counter-evidence, and shifts in my thinking.

**Topic:** $ARGUMENTS

## Steps

1. Search the entire vault for mentions of this topic and related concepts
2. Build a timeline of how my thinking on this has evolved
3. Look for:
   - Contradictions between what I said at different times
   - Counter-evidence I may be ignoring
   - Shifts in thinking I haven't acknowledged
   - Assumptions I'm making without evidence
   - Blind spots — what am I NOT writing about that's related?

## Output

Structure as:
1. **What you currently believe** (based on most recent vault entries)
2. **Where you contradicted yourself** (with dates and quotes)
3. **Counter-evidence from your own vault**
4. **What you might be avoiding**
5. **The steelman case against your current position**

Be direct. Don't be nice about it. The point is to strengthen my thinking by finding the weak points.
