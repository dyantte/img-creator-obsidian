# /drift — Detect intention vs. behavior gaps

Compare my stated intentions against my actual behavior over the last 30-60 days. Surface what I'm avoiding, what I'm over-indexing on, and where my actions don't match my words.

## Steps

1. Read all daily notes from the last 30-60 days in `Daily/`
2. Read project context files from `Projects/`
3. Look for:
   - Tasks I keep listing but never complete
   - Projects I say are important but haven't touched
   - Topics I claim to care about but never write about
   - Things I keep doing that aren't in any plan
   - Patterns in what I avoid vs. what I gravitate toward

## Output

1. **Stated priorities** (what the vault says I want to focus on)
2. **Actual behavior** (what the daily notes show I actually did)
3. **The drift** (where these diverge)
4. **What I'm avoiding** (things that keep getting pushed)
5. **What's pulling me** (things I keep doing even if unplanned)
6. **Honest assessment** — is the drift a problem, or is it my real priorities revealing themselves?

No judgment. Just pattern recognition. Let me decide what to do with it.
