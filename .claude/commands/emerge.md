# /emerge — Surface hidden ideas

Surface ideas the vault implies but never states directly. Look for conclusions from scattered premises, unnamed patterns, and unarticulated directions.

## Steps

1. Read the last 30-60 daily notes from `Daily/`
2. Read all project context files from `Projects/`
3. Follow backlinks and trace connections between notes
4. Look for:
   - Themes that keep appearing across different contexts but are never named
   - Ideas that are implied by combining notes from different domains
   - Patterns in what I keep coming back to
   - Directions my thinking is heading that I haven't articulated yet
   - Connections between projects that I haven't made explicit

## Output

For each emergent idea:
1. **The idea** (name it clearly — sometimes just naming it is the breakthrough)
2. **The evidence** (which notes and entries point to this)
3. **Why it matters** (what could I do with this insight)
4. **Confidence** (strong pattern vs. weak signal)

Surface 3-5 ideas. Quality over quantity. Only include ideas that would genuinely surprise me or that I haven't consciously articulated.
